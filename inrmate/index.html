Why I built it

For tracking and record keeping only. INR Mate does not provide medical advice or dosing recommendations.

Built from lived experience

Why I built INR Mate.

I’m Alistair Hutchinson, a UK-based Nurse Practitioner with over 25 years of healthcare experience, and I take warfarin long term after an aortic valve replacement.

I built INR Mate because I wanted a calmer, clearer way to keep my INR results, warfarin doses, reminders and notes organised, without pretending an app should replace clinical advice.

Tracking, not treatment.

INR Mate does not calculate your dose, diagnose problems, recommend treatment or replace your anticoagulation team. It is designed to help you keep better records and share clearer information.

30 May 2026 · INR Mate update

INR Mate v1.1 adds Comfort Mode, clinic visit PDFs and smoother sync

The first major update since launch focused on clearer everyday use, appointment-ready exports and smoother device syncing.

INR Mate v1.1 introduced Comfort Mode, an easier-to-read layout designed for larger text, clearer spacing and simpler day-to-day use.

It also added a clinic visit PDF, making it easier to prepare a clean summary of recent INR results, warfarin doses and notes for appointments.

Behind the scenes, v1.1 improved iPhone to Apple Watch syncing, polished the iPad experience, fixed smaller rough edges and kept the app moving forward based on early feedback.

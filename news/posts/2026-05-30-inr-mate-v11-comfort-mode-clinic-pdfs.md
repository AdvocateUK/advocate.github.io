INR Mate v1.1 is the first major update since launch, and it focuses on making the app easier to live with day to day.

Comfort Mode gives the interface a softer, calmer feel for people who prefer less visual intensity while tracking INR readings and warfarin doses.

Clinic visit PDFs make it easier to prepare a clear summary before appointments, helping users share recent INR results and warfarin records in a format that is simple to read.

The update also includes smoother syncing work across iPhone, iPad and Apple Watch, improving the foundations for future features.

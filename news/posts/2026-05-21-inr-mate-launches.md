INR Mate is now available on the App Store.

The app began as a personal project to solve a problem I knew well: keeping INR and warfarin records clear, accessible and useful over time.

It has grown into an app for iPhone, iPad and Apple Watch, with tools for recording INR results, tracking warfarin doses, reviewing recent history and exporting information when needed.

INR Mate is designed for record keeping only. Dosing decisions should always be made with the anticoagulation care team.

Thank you to everyone who helped test, gave feedback or encouraged the project along the way.
